About

A little about how I work

I'm a product designer and front-end developer based in the UK, working with clients across Europe, North America and Asia. My background blends visual design with hands-on engineering — I studied Computer Science with a focus on Human-Computer Interaction, then spent six years across in-house product teams and freelance studio work.

I care about the unglamorous details: load times, contrast ratios, the way a button feels on a trackpad versus a touchscreen. My goal on every project is the same — make something that looks considered, works everywhere, and earns the user's trust in the first five seconds.

Looking ahead, I'm focused on deepening my work in design systems and accessibility consulting, helping teams build products that scale visually and technically without losing their identity.
